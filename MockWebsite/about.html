Burning is made up of three band members. The leader of the band, who acts as its singer and bass player, is David Garcia. David created the band in 2007 and has since been its forntman and main song-writer.

The band's main guitarrist is Ronald Vanegas. Ronald has been playing the lead-guitar in multiple metal bands but since 2017 has been with Burning, helping them record their new album, Chains, Leather and Hell.

The third band member is Sebastian Baquiro. Sebastian has been playing the drums since 2010 when the bands previous drumer left in order to pursue personal projects.

HISTORY



Burning was created in 2007, when bassist David Garcia and a unnamed guitarist friend began to get together to play metal in David's garage. In 2010, after refining their skills as musicians, they recorded a bootleg sample of their songs, titled "Aggressive Possesion".

Burning then began to make small concerts in Metal bars in their hometown, Bogota, as well as neighboring cities such as Zipaquira, Villavicencio and even the small hot town of Honda.

Since their release of their album, Burning has been steadily releasing new material through the years, and making small tours around Colombia. Their latest proyect was to be released in Spotify, something that they achieved, having their latest album featured on the platform.
